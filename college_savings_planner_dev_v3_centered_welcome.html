Traveler's Tip

Time is powerful.

Every dollar invested earlier has more years to grow. Starting sooner often reduces the total amount you need to save because investment growth does more of the work. Time is one of the most valuable assets an investor has, and even modest amounts invested early can grow significantly through the power of compounding.
