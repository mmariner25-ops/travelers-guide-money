Planning Note: Income and costs are averaged over 12 months for planning purposes. Your college may bill by semester, quarter, or another schedule, so plan your cash flow accordingly.

This planner is designed around the goal of graduating without student loan debt whenever possible. Explore grants and scholarships, but avoid treating loans as “aid” in this cash-flow plan.
