Choosing a Retirement Income Goal

As a starting point, many people plan for retirement income that can support a lifestyle similar to their working years. However, your actual retirement needs may be higher or lower depending on your situation.

Some expenses may decrease, such as housing if your home is paid off, food if children are no longer living at home, commuting, or work-related expenses. Other expenses may increase, especially healthcare, travel, home maintenance, family assistance, or giving.
